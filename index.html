FAQs

What is the difference between The Hindu e-Paper and The Hindu Digital?

The Hindu e-Paper is an exact digital replica of the print newspaper, supercharged with the benefits of technology. Like the
print newspaper, it is curated and made available daily at 6:00 am and can be read at your convenience, in both web and
app formats. The Hindu digital is a live news platform (web and app) that is updated throughout the day with news articles,
videos, photo stories and podcasts. With the Hindu digital subscription, you can access all the premium articles and also get
ad free experience.

Can I use the same user ID and password across the platforms for accessing the content?

Yes. The same user ID and password will work across all the group applications.
